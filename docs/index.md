## Home

This is my attempt to build an Electronic Lab Notebook using the template designed by
Antoni Luque at the [Luquelab](https://www.luquelab.com). The template was based on recommendations from Noble PLoS Comp Biol 2009, Wilson et al PLoS Comp Biol 2017, and Briney "Data Management for Researchers" (2015), Hunt and Thomas "The Pragmatic Programmer: Your Journey to Mastery" (2019 2nd ed).
